Section 01

Introduction & Scope

KJP ALPHA ("we", "our", or "service") is an AI-powered chat assistant. This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains what information we collect about you when you use our platform, how we use it, and how we protect it.

By using this service, you consent to the terms of this Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this Policy, please do not use the service.

Section 03

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To process your AI chat requests and generate responses.
  • To improve service quality and conduct research for new features.
  • To detect and resolve technical issues.
  • To prevent abuse, spam, and inappropriate content.
  • To personalize the user experience (e.g., remembering preferred model).
  • To fulfill legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.
✅ Our Commitment We never sell your data or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

Section 04

Data Security Measures

We have implemented industry-standard technical and organizational measures to keep your data secure:

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses TLS 1.3 protocol encryption.
  • Local-First: Chat history stays in your device's localStorage, not on servers.
  • API Security: Requests sent to AI models are transmitted via secure channels.
  • Data Minimization: We only collect data necessary for providing the service.
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security audits are conducted to identify vulnerabilities.
⚠️ Limitations No data transmission or storage method on the internet is 100% secure. We strive for maximum protection, but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Section 06

Your Rights

As a user of our platform, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Delete Chats: You can permanently delete all your chat history via the "Delete All" button in the sidebar.
  • Data Portability: You can download your conversations using the PDF export feature.
  • Preference Control: You can change the model, toggle dark mode, and modify other settings at any time.
  • Right to Know: You can contact us to find out what information we hold about you.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your data for specific purposes.
ℹ️ Local Data Your chat history is stored in the browser — clearing browser data/cache or manually deleting from the sidebar will permanently erase it.

Section 07

Cookies & Local Storage

KJP ALPHA uses localStorage instead of browser cookies. Here are the details of locally stored data:

  • kjp-chats: All your chat sessions and message history.
  • kjp-model: Your selected AI model preference.
  • kjp-dark: Dark/light mode setting.
  • kjp-user: Profile information (name, email if entered).

All this data remains entirely on your device. You can delete it at any time from your browser settings.

💡 Regarding Google Fonts We use Google Fonts. When loading fonts, your IP address may be logged on Google's servers. For more information, please refer to Google's privacy policy.

🔒 For Parents Parental supervision is advised for teenagers aged 13–18 using this service.
